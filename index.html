Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels can have one or two humps
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand from getting into their eyes
  • Camels can go up to seven months in the desert without drinking water

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons mostly live in the rain forests and deserts of Africa
  • Chameleons have the fastest tongue speed

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
